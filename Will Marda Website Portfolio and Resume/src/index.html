About Me

Hi, I’m Will Marda, a passionate software developer with extensive experience in creating innovative solutions, ranging from web and mobile applications to video games. My journey in technology began in 2009, and since then, I’ve been dedicated to mastering programming languages like C#, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, as well as tools and frameworks such as GitHub, DevOps, Firebase, and Tailwind CSS.

I’ve worked in various roles, including as a contractor at Zerin Technologies, where I contributed to the Family Tools app, and as a teacher at Milisan School, helping young minds learn game development and animation. My experience also includes a long tenure as a Technical Support Engineer at BYU-Pathway Worldwide, ensuring seamless student experiences through bug verification and reporting.

Beyond work, I’m deeply committed to continuous learning. I have completed both an Associate's Degree and a Bachelor’s Degree in Software Development at BYU-Idaho, complementing my certificates in Web and Computer Programming, Web Development, and Software Development. My goal is to create impactful software that blends functionality with creativity.

Outside of coding, I enjoy exploring new technologies, mentoring aspiring developers, crafting personal projects that push the boundaries of innovation. I also paint miniatures for a game call Warhammer 40k and enjoy playing videogames and try to figure out how gameplay mechanics were coded and implemented. Let’s connect and build something amazing together!
